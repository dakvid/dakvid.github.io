Interatively Comparing Auckland’s Population Against Other Regions

How many regions does it take to match Auckland’s population? Here’s an interactive to explore the combinations.

David Friggens
2019-12-31

I recently came across Flooh Perlot’s impressive visualisation of Austrian election results highlighting the size of Graz. New Zealanders know that Auckland is big, and this is a good way of exploring the issue.

One of the benefits of creating the #30DayMapChallenge gallery is discovering gems that I missed in November.

Below you can see circles for the populations of the other 15 regions of New Zealand. Click on them, or click-and-drag a rectangle, to match them up against the population of Auckland region.

Data

  • 2019 estimated regional population from Stats NZ: population_rc.tsv
  • Simplified TopoJSON of regions: nzregions.topojson
